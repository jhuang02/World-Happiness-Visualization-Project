How is happiness influenced by various external factors around the world?

Team Members: Melody Zhu; Allen Su; Allison Gu; Jerry Huang


What makes us happy? As we transition through different stages of life, we slowly become more aware of our identity and our interests, and seek experiences that bring happiness to us individually--but what factors might be contributing to our happiness that we are not always explicitly aware of? Perhaps the country we live in, factors out of our control in our surroundings such as a country's socioeconomic status, government, culture, and other more subjective factors relative to the country's own citizens' opinions and perceptions are also contributing to a country's happiness. Through creating different visualizations out of data regarding happiness in different parts of the world, that incorporate different variables and factors, we hope to explore patterns and trends that may reveal insights about what increases or decreases a country's happiness index. We also hope that through our exploration, we may perhaps become more aware of our own happiness, how happiness can be measured objectively through happiness indexes, how happiness can also be subjective through our own perception of happiness, as well its place in our society and our community relative to others'.
