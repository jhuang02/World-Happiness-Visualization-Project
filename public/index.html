This interactive visualization allows users to manipulate the desired happiness score. Only countries within 0.4 of that level of happiness score will appear, and users should be able to see trends in factors such as social support, freedom, and corruption and how they affect a country's overall level of happiness. This is a rough visualization for now and other potential factors will be added in the future, along with tooltips for the dots revealing more info about the country.

This interactive visualization allows viewers to compare the Happiness Scores of different countries using a world map and determine trends from geographic location. Viewers can toggle through the years 2015 - 2020 and hovering over a country shows a bar graph visualizing how Freedom, Generosity, Government Trust, and Health scores contribute to the overall Happiness score.
